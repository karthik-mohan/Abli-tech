HEY THERE! WELCOME TO Abli-Tech

Abili-Tech Inc. is a translation app specializing in translations from Sign language to Spoken language and vice versa.

First translation app that is able to completely facilitate communication between oral language speakers and Sign language speakers.

The company’s based out of Canada, Abili-Tech aims to provide deaf/non-deaf people the ability to communicate with each other. Abili-Tech also seeks to reduce inequalities by improving communication.

It hopes to serve as a learning tool for people looking to learn Sign language.

Abili-Tech is looking forward to developing and scaling its app in Canada, where the infrastructure, access to technology, and talent pool are developed enough to establish a successful app.
